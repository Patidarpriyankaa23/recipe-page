Laddu

Laddu is a sweet, round treat made from flour, ghee, and sugar, loved across India for its rich taste and festive charm.

Prep Time: 20 min

Cook Time: 30 min

Serves: 8-10

Ingredients

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped dry fruits (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add the gram flour. Roast on low flame until golden brown and aromatic.
  2. Let the mixture cool slightly, then mix in powdered sugar and cardamom powder.
  3. Add dry fruits if using and mix well.
  4. Shape the mixture into small round laddus using your hands.
  5. Allow them to cool and store in an airtight container.

Nutrition (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 180
  • Fat: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 20g
  • Protein: 4g

Pro Tips

  • Roast the flour patiently for the best flavor.
  • Adjust sugar to your taste.
  • Ghee quality affects the taste significantly.
