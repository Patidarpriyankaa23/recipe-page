Rasmalai

Rasmalai is a soft, creamy dessert soaked in saffron milk and topped with crunchy nuts.

Prep Time: 30 min

Cook Time: 40 min

Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Few saffron strands
  • Chopped pistachios and almonds

Instructions

  1. Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle and form chenna. Strain and knead chenna.
  2. Shape into small balls and boil in sugar syrup for 15 minutes.
  3. Separately, boil 500ml milk, add sugar, saffron, and cardamom to make the ras (syrup).
  4. Soak the cooked balls in the cooled milk syrup for a few hours.
  5. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve chilled.

Nutrition (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 220
  • Protein: 6g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbs: 20g

Pro Tips

  • Use fresh full-cream milk for softer chenna.
  • Don’t overboil the chenna balls.
  • Let it chill overnight for best taste.
