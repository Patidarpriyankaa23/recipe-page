Paneer Tikka

Juicy chunks of marinated paneer grilled to perfection with veggies and spices, served with mint chutney and lemon.

Prep Time: 20 min

Cook Time: 15 min

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick curd/yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil or regular oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
  • 1 onion (cubed)
  • 1 capsicum (cubed)
  • Skewers or toothpicks (if using stove or oven)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix curd, ginger-garlic paste, all spices, lemon juice, and oil to prepare marinade.
  2. Add paneer cubes, onion, and capsicum to the marinade and coat well. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Skewer the marinated paneer and veggies alternately onto sticks.
  4. Grill on a pan, tandoor, or oven until charred and cooked evenly, turning occasionally.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney, lemon wedges, and onion rings.

Nutrition (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 280
  • Protein: 15g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Carbs: 10g

Pro Tips

  • Use thick curd to avoid a watery marinade.
  • Resting paneer in marinade enhances flavor.
  • Use mustard oil for smoky flavor (optional but authentic).
