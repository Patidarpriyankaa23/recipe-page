Palak Paneer

A creamy and flavorful North Indian dish made with pureed spinach and soft paneer cubes, seasoned with mild spices and herbs.

Prep Time: 15 min

Cook Time: 25 min

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 4 cups fresh spinach (palak)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cream (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Blanch spinach in hot water for 2 minutes, then transfer to cold water. Blend with green chilies into a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add onions and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  5. Add the spinach puree and simmer for 5 minutes. Add garam masala and cream (optional).
  6. Gently add paneer cubes, simmer for 3–4 minutes on low flame.
  7. Garnish with more cream or butter and serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.

Nutrition (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 270
  • Protein: 10g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Carbs: 12g

Pro Tips

  • Soak paneer in warm water to keep it soft.
  • Use fresh spinach for best flavor and vibrant color.
  • Add a pinch of kasuri methi for extra aroma.
