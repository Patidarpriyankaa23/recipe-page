Masala Dosa

A crispy South Indian rice-lentil crepe filled with spicy mashed potato filling, served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Prep Time: 8 hrs (including soaking and fermentation)

Cook Time: 30 min

Serves: 4

Ingredients

For Dosa Batter:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 tbsp poha (flattened rice)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

For Potato Filling:

  • 3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Chopped coriander (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Batter: Soak rice, urad dal, and poha for 4–6 hours. Grind into a smooth batter. Add salt and ferment overnight.
  2. Make Filling: Heat oil, crackle mustard seeds, add curry leaves, onions, green chili. Sauté. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes. Mix well. Garnish with coriander.
  3. Make Dosa: Heat a tawa, pour batter and spread into a thin circle. Drizzle oil on edges. Cook till crispy and golden.
  4. Place potato filling in the center, fold the dosa, and serve hot.

Nutrition (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 290
  • Carbs: 40g
  • Protein: 6g
  • Fat: 10g

Pro Tips

  • Use a well-seasoned tawa for crisp dosas.
  • Ferment batter in a warm place overnight for best results.
  • Serve with coconut chutney and sambar for authentic flavor.
