Fried Rice

A quick and tasty Indo-Chinese dish made with stir-fried rice, vegetables, and flavorful sauces — perfect for lunch or dinner.

Prep Time: 15 min

Cook Time: 15 min

Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked basmati rice (cooled)
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/2 cup chopped spring onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cabbage
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (preferably sesame or vegetable)
  • 2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan. Add chopped garlic and sauté for a few seconds.
  2. Add all the chopped vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 2–3 minutes. Keep them slightly crunchy.
  3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Add the cooked rice and gently toss everything together until well mixed and heated through.
  5. Garnish with spring onion greens and serve hot.

Nutrition (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 6g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Carbs: 45g

Pro Tips

  • Use day-old rice for best texture.
  • Do not overcook the vegetables — keep them crisp.
  • Adjust sauces based on spice level and personal preference.
